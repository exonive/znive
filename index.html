You’re free to experiment with techniques, try new technologies, or use what you know best, as long as it gets the job done. You’ll coordinate directly with the top-level management to set priorities and schedules.

In general, we expect you to be able to work for yourself and to help our company grow as best as you are able. Depending on your skills and performance, you will have chances to visit headquarters in Seattle.
